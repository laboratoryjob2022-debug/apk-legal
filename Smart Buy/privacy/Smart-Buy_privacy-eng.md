1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how NextGen Logic Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the "Smart Buy" mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data.
2. Personal Data Collection
The "Smart Buy" app does not collect, store, or share any personal user data such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location. All price and weight calculations are performed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any servers.

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Yandex Mobile Ads SDK: We show advertisements using Yandex SDK. Yandex may collect anonymous technical data (such as device identifiers, IP address, and hardware information) to provide relevant ads.

AppMetrica: We use AppMetrica for anonymous analytics to improve the app's performance and stability.
4. Internet Access
The app requires internet access primarily to load advertisements and send anonymous analytics.
5. User Rights and Data Protection
Since we do not collect personal data, we do not store any information about you on our servers. You have full control over the app's data by clearing the app cache or uninstalling it.
